§ 329-1. Composition.
[Amended 2011-06-15 by By-law 773-2011; 2019-01-31 by By-law 255-20192]
There shall be 11 members of the Board of Management of the Toronto Zoo, one (1) of whom
shall be the Mayor or a designate appointed by the Mayor, and ten (10) others who shall be
appointed by the Council of the City of Toronto as follows:
(A)
Two (2) shall be Members of Council, and
(B)
Eight (8) shall be members of the public.

and fourteen (14) others who shall be appointed by the Council of the City of Toronto as follows:
(A)
Six (6) shall be Members of Council, and

§ 329-3. Term of appointment.
[Added 2011-06-15 by By-law 773-2011]
Other than the Mayor, members of the Board who are Members of Council shall serve for a term
of 24 months, or until such earlier or later time as their replacement is appointed by Council. In
the event the Mayor appoints a designate, the designate shall serve for the term of Council or
until such earlier time as the Mayor appoints a replacement designate or appoints himself.
Members of the Board who are members of the public shall serve for the term set by Council, or
until their replacement is appointed.
§ 329-4. Transition, Council Member appointments.
[Added 2011-06-15 by By-law 773-2011]
Despite § 329-3, the six (6) members of Council appointed pursuant to § 329-2 shall serve until
December 31, 2012, or until such later time as Council appoints the three (3) members of
Council to the Board of Management of the Toronto Zoo for a term commencing from or after
December 31, 2012.
§ 329-5. Sponsorship and naming policies.
[Added 2011-12-01 by By-law 1375-2011]
The Board of Management of the Toronto Zoo shall adopt policies respecting sponsorships,
naming rights and honourific names which are consistent with the policies adopted by Council.
