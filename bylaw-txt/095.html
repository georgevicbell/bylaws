ARTICLE I
Membership
[Adopted 1998-02-06 by By-law No. 27-19981]
§ 95-1. Definitions.
As used in this article, the following terms shall have the meanings indicated:
MUNICIPAL MEMBER - A person appointed to the Board of Health by Council.
§ 95-2. Number of members.
[Amended 1999-02-04 by By-law No. 18-1999]
The Board of Health shall consist of 13 municipal members.
§ 95-3. Term of office.
The term of office of a municipal member continues during the pleasure of Council that
appointed the municipal member but, unless ended sooner, ends with the ending of the term of
office of the Council.
