ARTICLE I
Reward for Information Leading to False Fire Alarm Conviction
[Adopted 2000-05-11 by By-law No. 259-20001]
§ 433-1. Amount of reward.
A reward of $1,000 shall be offered and, if accepted, paid to any person who supplies
information leading to the conviction of any person or persons who wilfully, without reasonable
cause, make or circulate or cause to be made or circulated an alarm of fire within the City of
Toronto.
§ 433-2. Source of reward money.
The money payable to such persons shall be paid from the revenues generated from the False
Alarm account currently being managed by the Fire Service.
§ 433-3. Posting of signs by landlords.
Landlords shall be requested to post an appropriate sign above the fire alarm citing this article
and the reward.
